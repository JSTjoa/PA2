In this case, although there is a certificate, the intruder can just record and replay/playback the message to request for connection to the Server. Server cannot tell if the incoming message comes from Client or the intruder. This is called the replay/playback attack.
By implementing a one-time nonce, the intruder would not be able to do the replay/playback attack.
